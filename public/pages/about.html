Common Questions

Questions we get
asked most

If your question isn't here, just get in touch and we'll answer it directly.

How much does a website cost?

Landing pages start from £499. Business websites start from £899. Growth websites from £1,299. Every project gets a fixed quote upfront; what we quote is what you pay. No hourly billing, no scope creep charges.

How long does it take?

Most projects go from first call to live website in 7 to 14 days. The exact timeline depends on complexity and how quickly you can provide content and feedback. We'll give you a clear schedule before we start.

Do I need to provide the content?

Ideally yes, you know your business better than anyone. We will guide you with a content brief so you know exactly what's needed. If you'd prefer, we can draft copy as an add-on service.

What about hosting and domains?

You own and pay for your own hosting and domain. We will advise on the best options and help you set everything up. Usually £5–£15 per month. You are never tied to us, no ongoing fees from our side.

Do you work with businesses outside Dorset?

Absolutely. We're based in Poole, Dorset, and work with small businesses across the UK. Everything is done remotely: calls, reviews, and handover, so your location is never a barrier.

What if I need changes after launch?

Every project includes a round of revisions before launch. For changes after, just get in touch and we'll give you a clear, fixed price for any additional work.

Will my site show up on Google?

All our sites are built with solid on-page SEO foundations: clean structure, proper headings, fast loading, and mobile-friendly. We set up Google Search Console on launch day. Long-term SEO growth takes time, but you start on the right foot.
