About the Studio

A studio built on
quiet excellence.

Velvet Room Studio has been Sherborne's home for premium hair since 2017. Founded by senior stylist Claire Alderton, the studio was built on a single belief: that every client deserves a result they're genuinely proud of — not just on the day, but every day after.

We keep our team small, our appointments unhurried, and our standards exceptionally high.

Fully tailored, every time

No two clients are the same. Every appointment begins with a proper consultation so we understand exactly what you want and what will work for your hair.

Premium products, always

We use only professional-grade colour and care products — kinder to your hair, and built to last so your results stay beautiful for longer.

Your time, respected

We run to time, keep our space calm, and make sure every visit feels like a treat — not a rushed appointment squeezed into a busy schedule.
