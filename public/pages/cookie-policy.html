What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to remember your preferences.

What cookies we use

We use exactly one cookie on this website:

  • hs_cookie_consent — This is a functional cookie that stores your response to our cookie notice (accepted or declined). It has no expiry date set, which means it persists until you clear your browser storage. It contains no personal data — only the word "accepted" or "declined".

We do not use any tracking, advertising, analytics, or third-party cookies of any kind. No data from this cookie is shared with any third party.

What we do not use cookies for

  • Tracking your behaviour across websites
  • Advertising or remarketing
  • Analytics or performance monitoring
  • Social media integration

Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies at any time through your browser settings. Doing so will not affect your ability to use this website — the only effect is that the cookie consent banner will reappear on your next visit.

For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, visit aboutcookies.org.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how we use cookies, this page will be updated and the date at the top will reflect the change.

Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, contact us at hello@harbourstudios.uk.
