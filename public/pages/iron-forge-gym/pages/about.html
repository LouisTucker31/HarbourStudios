Our Story

BUILT BY ATHLETES,
FOR EVERYONE

Founded in 2017, IronForge started with a single warehouse, two coaches and an obsession with results. Today we're London's most respected independent gym.

Who We Are

MORE THAN A GYM

IronForge was born out of frustration with gyms that didn't deliver. Our founders, both competitive athletes, wanted a space that combined elite-level equipment with expert coaching and a genuinely supportive community.

Eight years later, we've trained over 10,000 members, produced national-level competitors, and built a culture where everyone from beginners to pros trains side by side.

We don't do gimmicks. We do hard work, smart programming and real results.

What Drives Us

OUR VALUES

⚡

Intensity with Purpose

Every session has a goal. We train with intention, not just to sweat, but to progress. Smart intensity beats mindless effort every time.

🤝

Community First

Your gym is only as good as the people in it. We've built a culture where everyone supports each other, at every level.

📈

Progress Over Perfection

We celebrate consistent effort. Small wins compound into massive transformations. Show up, put in the work, trust the process.

🧠

Education-Led Coaching

Every trainer is certified, experienced and constantly learning. We explain the why behind the work so you can train smarter.

🛡️

Safety Without Compromise

We never let egos override safety. Proper form, progressive loading and injury prevention are non-negotiable in every session.

🔓

Inclusive by Design

Elite results are for everyone. Whether you're 18 or 68, beginner or advanced, IronForge has a programme that works for you.
