About TrustFlow

Dorchester's trusted local plumbers since 2012. Gas Safe registered, fully insured, and committed to doing the job right first time.

Our Story

Built on reputation,
run on trust.

TrustFlow Plumbing was founded in 2012 by James Carter, a qualified plumber with over 20 years of experience in the trade. After years working for larger contractors, James wanted to build something different — a local business where every customer gets the same high standard of work, clear communication, and genuine respect for their home.

Twelve years on, TrustFlow has grown to a team of five engineers covering Dorchester and the wider Dorset area. We have completed over 2,400 jobs — from emergency call-outs to full bathroom installations — and our 4.9-star Google rating reflects what we care about most: doing excellent work and leaving every customer happy.

We are a small, local business and our reputation is everything to us. That is why every job, no matter the size, gets our full attention and a fixed, transparent price agreed before we start.
